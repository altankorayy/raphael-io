Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages

  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

In addition, when you choose to upload your own photo for image generation:

  • Your uploaded photo is stored securely by the Application to avoid repeated uploads on subsequent uses.
  • The stored photo is only accessed by the Application when generating a new AI-based image.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application collects your device's location, which helps the Service Provider determine your approximate geographical location and make use of in below ways:

  • Geolocation Services: The Service Provider utilizes location data to provide features such as personalized content, relevant recommendations, and location-based services.
  • Analytics and Improvements: Aggregated and anonymized location data helps the Service Provider to analyze user behavior, identify trends, and improve the overall performance and functionality of the Application.
  • Third-Party Services: Periodically, the Service Provider may transmit anonymized location data to external services. These services assist them in enhancing the Application and optimizing their offerings.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to UserID. The information that the Service Provider request will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.

Face Data and User Photos

The Application includes features that utilize facial data and user-uploaded photos. Facial data (image scans, feature maps) is considered biometric data under applicable law:

  • Legal Basis & Consent: We process your biometric data only after obtaining your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent at any time via the app’s Help Center or by emailing altankoraybenli@gmail.com.
  • Local Processing: Any face data captured in real-time (e.g., camera preview) is processed solely on your device and is not retained.
  • Uploaded Photo Storage: Photos you upload for avatar or image generation are stored on Firebase Storage under the default security settings provided by Firebase. These are used only to streamline repeat usage and are not shared with third parties except as required for AI processing.
  • Transmission to AI Service: To generate your custom image, we share the URL of your stored photo with our AI image generation service. Only the photo URL is transmitted; no additional metadata or personal identifiers are shared.
  • Access, Correction & Erasure: You have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your face data and photos. Submit a request via the app’s Help Center or by contacting us at altankoraybenli@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
  • Security Controls: We rely on Firebase’s security measures for data protection, including access controls and encrypted connections.
  • Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach affecting facial data, we will notify affected users and authorities (e.g., KVKK, GDPR regulators) as required by law.
  • Children’s Biometric Data: We do not knowingly process biometric data of children under 13. For users aged 13–16, we obtain parental consent and provide additional transparency measures.
