Stay Hydrated, Stay Healthy!

Tracking your daily water intake has never been easier. Our app helps you develop one of the most important habits for a healthy lifestyle with personalized water intake calculations and detailed beverage tracking. Whether you're just starting or already have a regular water consumption routine, our smart tracking system offers solutions tailored to you.

Comprehensive Beverage Tracking:

Calculate not just water, but the water content in all your beverages to easily see how close you are to your daily goal. From coffee to tea, juice to smoothies, we automatically calculate the water value of everything you drink, making it simple to stay on top of your hydration goals.

Smart Widgets & Quick Actions:

Monitor your daily water intake at a glance with our interactive widgets. View both the amount and percentage of your daily goal without opening the app, and log your water intake directly from the widget. Stay informed and in control with just a tap!

Apple Health Integration:

Seamlessly sync your hydration data with Apple Health. Every drink you log is automatically recorded in your health data, giving you a complete picture of your wellness journey across all your devices.

Personalized Reminders:

Never forget to hydrate again! Set custom reminder intervals that work with your schedule. Receive motivational notifications during your chosen time slots to help you maintain consistent hydration throughout the day.

Your Preferences, Your Way:

Choose your preferred unit of measurement (ml, oz) and customize the app to match your needs. Our flexible settings ensure you can track your hydration in the way that works best for you.

Stay on Track with Real-Time Progress:

Watch your daily progress with clear, visual feedback. See exactly how much you've consumed and how far you are from your daily goal with our intuitive interface. Understanding and maintaining your hydration has never been more straightforward!
